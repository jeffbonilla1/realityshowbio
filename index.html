About

Jeff Bonilla is a retired law enforcement officer, published author, and produced songwriter who creates unscripted television that get optioned. He knows the difference between a concept that sounds good in a room and one that actually works on screen.

His series have been acquired by Ryan Seacrest Productions, Jamie Kennedy Entertainment, Merv Griffin Productions, 3 Ball Productions, Bunim-Murray, and Nitelite Productions.

He is currently developing a new slate of concepts built for the streaming era — emotionally resonant, visually distinctive, and commercially viable.

Debtonator

"What if instead of winning money... you destroyed your debt?"

Solo contestants or families compete in seven rounds of increasingly difficult, debt-themed physical and mental challenges. Win all seven and push the Debtonator handle down — obliterating their debt in a spectacular explosion of confetti and shredded bills. $50,000 in debt elimination. Real people. Real stakes. Real catharsis.

Money Mountain

"Six teams. Locked backpacks. One contains $100,000. Only one team will find out."

Teams of two race up a mountain through grueling obstacle courses while carrying padlocked backpacks — only one of which contains cold hard cash. Runners tackle the course. Gunners man water cannons to slow opponents. The original format that proved the market.

BandWarp

"Spin the wheel. Enter the door. Become a completely different band."

Unknown bands compete for a record contract — but there's a catch. They spin a wheel that sends them through a door into a completely different musical era. The death metal band that has to write doo-wop. The boy band forced to play the blues. Every episode is a different world.
